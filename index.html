Measure Dashboard
Nomi’s campaign watching software allows Campaign Managers to see how effective their campaigns are by showing what percentage of their customers are coming back after a campaign and become loyal customers after that. It also shows what percentage of visitors in the past 30 day attribution period have come back & have been “reactivated” by an on-going campaign.
