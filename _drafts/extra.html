Lane Management Dashboard For Front-end managers at grocery and retail stores we designed a dashboard for them to use both while walking around the store and on-screen version that shows them how many checkout lanes they need to open based off of the line lengths.

Nomi Beacon Demo app The Marketing team & I collaborated to create an in-store experience within a trade show for a “Store of the Future”. In it the user would be able to interact with beacon equipped posters & merchandise. The posters displayed a promotion for a chance to win a trip to one of the 3 biggest sporting events of the season. Meanwhile interacting with the merchandise with a tap by a user’s phone would pull up the details about that product & a discount on that product.

Nomi Operations Dashboard Nomi’s most sought after product is the Operations Dashboard. Where we display the traffic data we collect from our cameras & sensors to COOs & store managers for them to be able to get analysis on displays, optimize opening & closing hours & when to put more associates on the floor based off of foot traffic.

Nomi Marketing Dashboard Nomi’s campaign watching software allows Campaign Managers to see how effective their campaigns are by showing what percentage of their customers are coming back after a campaign and become loyal customers after that. It also shows what percentage of visitors in the past 30 day attribution period have come back & have been “reactivated” by an on-going campaign. For this I designed a delightful campaign creation workflow for the Campaign managers that walks them through the various options for watching their events.

Floorplan Report The store view is the store manager’s dashboard for what’s going on in their store. They can upload their floor plan & see where cameras & sensors are installed & how many people pass by or “dwell" in specific areas. There is also a Movement feature that shows the user what percentage of visitors go from one section of the store to another.
