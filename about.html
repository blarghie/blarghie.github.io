Hi! Thank you for checking out my work.

I'm a traditionally trained Graphic Designer. I went to school in Philadelphia, PA after falling in love with graphic design in a college in the Philippines — where I grew up. I watched a lot of anime and read a lot of manga which made me want to try to make a career out of being able to draw. In Art School (University of the Arts) besides drawing and painting on sketchbooks and newsprint I focused on interactive media, mostly digital, mostly in Flash and Actionscript. I learned to code from creating digital solutions to design problems — making round characters bounce around the screen by interacting with it.

I attribute working in media now from a project in school where I plotted to create a programmable news network that integrated with a users' cable box. It was inspired by people's morning routine and and need of information while multi tasking at getting ready for work or school. I like coming up with solutions on how to consume different kinds of information.

I think I've worked with an amazing team of engineers, product managers and designers. Find them here.
